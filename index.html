Spin Riksone

SPIN Riksone Design, Inc. was founded in 1998 by owner, Javier ESPIN Riksone, to be your one stop shop for:
Toy Design / Product Management / Package Design Illustration / Concept Development / Factory
Spin Ricksone S.L. has developed products for clients such as Netflix, Fornite, Call of Duty, Cartoon Network a variety of todays hottest and largest brands. Collaborating on many projects ranging from promotions to packaging, character design to retail and collectible toys, Spin Riksone is ready to take on your next project. Aside from commercial projects, owner Javier Espín has also worked non-stop on his own licensed toy lines and brands under the Spin Rickson Design label. His debut brand the SPINK™ has been a staple platform in the designer toy market since it first released in 1998
